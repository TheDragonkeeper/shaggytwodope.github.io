About

About Sober Grad

Sober Grad Night offers seniors and their guests a variety of all night fun and entertaining activities that include:

  • Lots of food and beverages free of charge
  • Karaoke
  • Interactive Inflatable Games
  • 3 DJ's
  • Mechanical Bull
  • Casino
  • Pictures
  • Free Raffles
  • Temporary Tattoos
  • Live Music Artists


Who Organizes The Event?
Sober Grad Night, Inc. is a non-profit organization run 100% by volunteers. The volunteers are parents, community members, business people, etc. Who care about making graduation fun, memorable, and safe for the Seniors and their guests. A small group of 6 to 10 people plan and organize the event activities, but it takes approximately 200 volunteers to set up and run the various activities the night of the event. Funding for Sober Grad Night comes solely from community donations and ticket sales.

Volunteer

Sober Grad Nite Volunteers

Volunteers are always needed. Sober Grad Night does not happen without community participation. It takes approximately 200 volunteers to make it happen. There are a variety of volunteer jobs to be filled in order for Sober Grad Night to be a success. Advance organizing, fund raising and prize procurement is done far ahead of time. Decorating and set up occurs the afternoon of the event. Many adult volunteers are needed during the event to keep all the activities going. Some volunteers may work a single shift and others stay the whole night.

Please consider volunteering for the worthy cause. Use the Contact section below.
